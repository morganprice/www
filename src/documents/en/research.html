We provide specialist services in data management and research management, as well as technical support and development for grant-funded projects in science and technology, the humanities and arts.

Among the areas in which we have particular expertise, we can advise on:

  • image, sound & media services, digital curation & metadata
  • online publishing & journals, peer review administration
    • Open Journal Systems
    • HyperJournal
  • digital rights management, intellectual property & copyright
  • data management solutions & software platforms
  • research information management & specialist software
  • DSpace, EPrints, CKAN, DKAN etc.

We have experience working with national UK funders and RCUK, e.g. national UK strategy in research identifiers, grant proposals and more. We are happy to discuss your needs for large or small projects.
